ABOUT GALXBOY

Galxboy is a South African streetwear brand built for a generation that refuses to blend in.

Our Story

Born From the Ground Up

Born and raised from the raw energy of youth culture, the brand was created for those individuals who like to challenge the norm and carve their own path.

Vision

Freedom Through Fashion

To empower individuals to express themselves freely and confidently through fashion — on their own terms, in their own way.

Mission

SA Street Culture, Global Stage

To take South African street culture to the global stage and show the world what it means to move different.

What We Stand For

Authenticity  ·  Confidence  ·  Creativity  ·  Individuality
