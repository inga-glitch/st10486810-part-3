1.ORGANASATIONAL OVERVIEW & USER PERSONA 
Galxboy is a South African streetwear brand owned by Thatiso Dube which has its roots 
in the underground and rebellious youth street culture that dominates that urban centre 
of Mzansi. In 2008 while Thatiso was still in high school he saw a gap in the market for a 
brand that was designed by the youth for the youth in particular millennials as well as 
Generation Z. It did not just stop there, through ambition, perseverance and teamwork 
Galxboy was born. 
WHAT IS GALXBOY VISION AND MISSION STATEMENT? 
Galxboy vision is it aims to become the leading streetwear brand on the african 
continent. It also aims to inspire the youth through creative design, innovation, and 
affordable pricing driven by the brands mantra of “SMART. AFRICAN. AMBITIOUS.” 
(GALXBOY 2025)

5. DEVELOPMENT TOOLS 
Development Tools / Technology Stack 
The development of the Galxboy website will be based on fundamental front-end web 
technologies, namely HTML5 and CSS3. These technologies are widely recognised as 
standard tools for structuring and designing modern websites. 
HTML5 (HyperText Markup Language) will be used to define the structure and semantic 
layout of the website. It will handle organizing content elements such as headings, images, 
navigation menus, and product information across various pages, including the homepage and 
product catalogue. 
CSS3 (Cascading Style Sheets) will be applied to control the visual presentation and layout 
of the website. This includes the use of color schemes, typography, spacing, and responsive 
design techniques to ensure that the website is visually appealing and accessible across 
different devices. CSS3 will also play a key role in setting up a consistent brand identity that 
reflects the modern streetwear aesthetic of Galxboy. 
Together, HTML5 and CSS3 will provide a structured, responsive, and visually cohesive user 
interface, contributing to an effective and engaging user experience.
